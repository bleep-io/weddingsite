Our Special Day

Order of the Day

Main Ceremony

1:00 PM 2:00 PM
Saturday 22nd August, 2020

We’ll be saying ‘I do’ in the rustic ‘Tithe Barn’ during our civil ceremony. With its flagstone floors, oak beams and high ceilings the barn really evokes the same character and atmosphere as church. Throw in some fairy lights too and it has a truly magical feel.

Reception

1:30 PM 4:00 PM
Saturday 22 August, 2020

After the ceremony, we’ll be heading out the courtyard or, weather dependent (we’re up north after all!), we’ll head up to the garden terrace in front of the grand Browsholme Hall to relax and celebrate. If you’re feeling energetic, you can join in some fun garden games!

Wedding Breakfast

4:00 PM 6:00 PM
Saturday 22nd August, 2020

After all the fun and games you’ll be ready for our Wedding Breakfast. We’ll be heading back to the barn for an informal meal – we’ve decided against a traditional three-course meal and we won’t be having a top table!

The Wedding Party

7.00 PM 12.30 AM
Saturday 22 August, 2020

Let the drunken antics and dad-dancing begin! During the evening, Manchester-based band, The Cave, will be getting the party started with two 60-minute “rock and pop electric” sets and DJ-ing in between. The dance floor must be occupied at all times. All skill levels accepted. Dance-offs are welcome.

Our Story

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts.

  • First We Meet

    November, 2016

    It’s hard to pin point when we first met as we both worked in the same office! I started to notice this geeky data guy who suddenly appeared in the office. We’d never spoke but there was something about him and I knew I wanted to get to know him. Eventually our paths crossed, and our romance blossomed over a data project (it was exciting as it sounds!).

  • Our First Date

    September 8, 2017

    Why settle for the cinema or a meal when you can have a romantic weekend away in Edinburgh for your first date! The first of many little trips and adventures we’ve had together so far – and Nick’s first taste of my fussy eating after spending an hour walking through the streets of Edinburgh looking for somewhere I would eat!

  • It's Facebook Official!

    September 23, 2017

    So, the first date sealed the deal! Less than 2 weeks later I popped the question (not that one!) on Nick’s birthday. With our shared love of penguins, we headed to Chester Zoo

  • He Popped the Question

    September 19, 2018

    Climbing to the top of our castle tower with high heels was far from fun, but it was worth it. As we were sat, legs dangling off the edge of the tower, watching the sun set over the Adriatic Sea, he asked me to spend the rest of his life him!
